Fifth Avenue

Fifth Avenue is a street in downtown Manhattan in New York; one of the most famous, respectable and expensive streets in the world, where there are many exclusive boutiques. Fifth Avenue starts at Washington Square Park in Greenwich Village and heads north through Midtown along the east side of Central Park, through the Upper East Side and Harlem, and ends at the Harlem River. The abundance of architectural monuments, historical and cultural centers, fashion boutiques and exclusive shops is concentrated on this New York street.
